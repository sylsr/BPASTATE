Amusement Park

On our adventure through California, we will be stopping through various amusement parks. Disneyland is one of the parks we will be visiting. The world famous Disneyland Park includes world famous attractions in the place known as “The happiest place on earth.” Another park that we will be visiting is Knott’s Berry Farm. This park is known for its crazy rollercoasters perfect for the inner adrenaline junky. We will be visiting these parks and many more.

Rooms

Rooms: We will be staying at various hotels close to the amusement parks and hotspots there will be complimentary transportation to the amusement parks. The vacationer will be able to choose from various hotels around the parks that will meet your needs and make your journey through California enjoyable.

Shows

While on your California journey, various theatrical shows will be available depending on the time of your adventure. Some of the shows available are shows such as, Wicked, Matilda, and many more. These shows offer fun for the whole family.

Tours of California

On the tour of California we will be visiting various hot spots in California. One of the many different spots we will visit is the Hollywood Walk of Fame. At the Walk of Fame, you will be able to see the stars of acclaimed celebrities.

Sporting Events

While on your adventure, we will stop by some various sporting events in the California area, depending on the time of your journey. Some of the sporting teams we visit are the Anaheim Angels, LA Dodgers, LA Clippers, and many more.

Celebrity Tour

We will be going on a celebrity tour of Los Angeles. This tour will go through Los Angeles and will show you the homes of various celebrities ranging from movie stars to musicians. Some of the celebrity houses that we will visit include Halle Berry, Bruno Mars, Bruce Willis, Simon Cowell, and many more.

Beaches

There are many glorious beaches to discover in California. This tour will visit some of the famous beaches in California. Some of the beaches we will visit include Laguna Beach, Venice Beach, Santa Monica, and many more.
