Private Beach

On your Bahamian getaway, we offer a private island with pristine beaches and many of activities to do. You can relax with our shore side spa, or you could indulge in our fresh seafood. Or you can enjoy our lagoon-style pool with stellar poolside seating and a swim up bar.

Dining Opportunities

This resort comes included with many different dining options and different styles of cuisine. We have seafood restaurants, French cuisine, Italian restaurants, and many more to discover.

Relaxing Environment

One of our goals is that every person has a fun, relaxing time at our Resort. Our staff works hard to ensure that the environment is as relaxing as possible with rooms that lookout to the peaceful waters and employees that are available to make sure that your stay is all that you hope it to be and more.

Pools/Spa

This resort includes the option to enjoy our Red Lane Spa. Here we offer couples massages and aromatherapy massages. We also offer facials, hot stone therapy, foot massages, massages for expecting mothers, and many more. Also, we have many different pools and beaches to have all the fun in the water that you want. We have pools with swim-up bars, scuba diving, and many other fun ways to enjoy the pristine water in this beautiful environment.

Rooms

We have many different room types to fit any budget and needs. The base level of rooms are called the Luxury Level Rooms. These rooms include luxurious and calming furniture with a private balcony with an ocean view. The next rooms are called the Club Level Rooms. These rooms offer a stunning view of the beach and ocean. Included is a luxurious dining and sleeping area with fine furniture, top-of-the-range television, and a private balcony with breathtaking views. The next rooms are called the Butler Level Rooms. These rooms are extravagantly decorated that lookout to the ocean. Included is a large living area, dining area, and sleeping area. Also, these rooms have a top-of-the-range television, and a luxurious bathroom with a large tub. 24-hour room service is included, as well as, VIP Rolls Royce or Mercedes airport transfers and exclusive dining at our various restaurants.

Gym

Our gyms are free to use for the vacationers and offer 24/7 access, complimentary instruction, and many more. We are equipped with treadmills, elliptical, exercise bikes, and stair climbers all with iPod docks with the capacity to watch videos or play music. Also, we have yoga, aerobics, Zumba, Pilates classes, and many more complimentary classes.

Friendly Staff

Our staff is determined to make sure that your stay is as relaxing and enjoyable as possible. We have staff standing by 24/7 to make sure that all your needs are met and that your stay allows you to relax and enjoy the time you have with your family in this island utopia.
